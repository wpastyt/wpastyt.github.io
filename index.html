1. Click the "Earn Coin" button in the top navigation

2. You'll receive 1 coin instantly, this will open a new tab (that is basically advertisement so you can close that tab)

3. Wait 15 seconds before you can earn again

4. Repeat as many times as you want to collect coins

5. Coins reset when you refresh the page
